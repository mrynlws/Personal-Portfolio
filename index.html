About Me


Hello! My name is Marianne Louise Buama, but you can call me Yannie. I'm a 4th-year student at FEU Alabang, currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science with specialization in Software Engineering. I am an Events Committee Associate of Google Developer Student Clubs FEU Alabang, where I previously held the position of Vice President External. Additionally, I am a member of Association for Computing Machinery at my university and previously served as an Auditor. I take great pride in my academic achievements and I am proud to consistently be awarded as a top-performing student. Because of this, I am a recipient of the President's Scholarship and have been inducted into the Honor Society.
