About Me


Hello! My name is Marianne Louise Buama, though I prefer to be called Yannie. I hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science with a specialization in Software Engineering from FEU Alabang. I graduated Magna Cum Laude and received a Leadership award. Previously, I served as an Events Committee Associate at Google Developer Student Clubs FEU Alabang, where I held the position of Vice President External. Additionally, I was a member of the Association for Computing Machinery at my university, where I previously served as an Auditor. I take considerable pride in my academic accomplishments and am honored to have been consistently recognized as a top-performing student. As a result of my achievements before entering college, I was awarded the President's Scholarship and inducted into the Tertiary Honor Society. I was also a scholar under the Muntinlupa Scholarship Division.
